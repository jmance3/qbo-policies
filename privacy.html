Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Internal Use Only: This application is designed exclusively for internal use by South Fork Design Build and is not distributed to external users or third parties.

1. Application Purpose

This QuickBooks integration ("the Application") is developed and maintained by South Fork Design Build for internal business operations only. The Application connects to QuickBooks Online to facilitate:

  • Invoice generation and management
  • Financial data retrieval and reporting
  • Project-related accounting tasks
  • Internal workflow automation

2. Information Collection and Use

The Application accesses the following data from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Customer and vendor information
  • Invoices, estimates, and bills
  • Chart of accounts
  • Items and services
  • Financial transactions

Data Storage: All data remains within:

  • QuickBooks Online (primary storage)
  • Local development environment (temporary processing only)
  • Google Drive (for invoice and document storage)

3. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • OAuth 2.0: Secure authentication via Intuit's official OAuth protocol
  • Token Management: Access tokens are stored locally and refreshed automatically
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Data is never shared with external parties
  • Local Processing: All operations occur within our controlled environment

4. Data Retention

This Application does not maintain permanent copies of QuickBooks data. All queries fetch real-time data from QuickBooks Online. Generated documents (invoices, reports) are stored in designated Google Drive folders for business record-keeping purposes.

5. User Access

Access to this Application is restricted to:

  • Jeff Mance (Principal, South Fork Design Build)
  • Authorized South Fork Design Build team members only

6. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online: Subject to Intuit's Privacy Policy
  • Google Drive/Gmail: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Notion: Subject to Notion's Privacy Policy

7. Data Rights

As the QuickBooks account owner, you retain full control over your data:

  • Access, modify, or delete data within QuickBooks Online at any time
  • Revoke Application access via QuickBooks Online settings
  • Request data deletion by disconnecting the Application
